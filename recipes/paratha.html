Paratha

Description:

A delicious and versatile Indian flatbread enjoyed as a breakfast item or accompaniment to meals.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • Water (as needed)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or ghee
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour, salt, and oil/ghee.
  2. Add water gradually and knead the mixture into a smooth, pliable dough.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle.
  4. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat and place a rolled dough circle on it.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown spots appear.
  6. Repeat the process with the remaining dough balls.
  7. Brush the cooked parathas with additional ghee if desired and serve hot with your favorite side dish.
